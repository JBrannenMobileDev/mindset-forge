How does the free trial work?

New subscribers get a 7-day free trial with full access to MindsetForge. You will not be charged until the trial ends. If you cancel before the trial is over, you will not be billed.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

iPhone / iPad: Open the Settings app, tap your name at the top, then tap Subscriptions and select MindsetForge to change or cancel your plan.

Android: Open the Google Play Store app, tap your profile icon, then go to Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions and select MindsetForge.

Cancelling at least 24 hours before the renewal date stops the next charge.

How do I restore a purchase?

If you reinstalled the app or switched devices, open MindsetForge, go to the subscription screen, and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID or Google account you used to subscribe.

How do I delete my account and data?

You can delete your account at any time from the in-app Settings screen. This permanently removes your profile and all associated data. For full details and an email request option, see our account deletion page.

Is MindsetForge a substitute for therapy?

No. MindsetForge is a mindset and personal-growth coach. It is not therapy, counseling, or medical care, and is not a substitute for advice from a licensed mental-health professional. If you are in crisis, contact 988 by call or text, or your local emergency services.
