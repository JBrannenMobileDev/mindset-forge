## ASO strategy (read first)

App Store search weights the **App Name** > **Subtitle** > a hidden
**Keywords** field. The description does NOT feed search, so it is free to be
pure persuasion. That means you do not have to sacrifice a premium, distinctive
name/subtitle to rank: put the boring high-volume search terms in the hidden
keywords field, and keep the visible copy sharp.

Apple de-duplicates: do not repeat words already used in the App Name or
Subtitle inside the Keywords field (they are already indexed). Validate the
proposed keyword volumes in App Store Connect search or an ASO tool before
publishing.

## Keywords field (<=100 chars, hidden, comma-separated, no spaces after commas)

`self,improvement,habit,tracker,discipline,motivation,journal,affirmations,manifestation,confidence`  (98)

Notes:
- Single-word tokens let Apple auto-combine them ("self improvement", "habit
  tracker", "self discipline", etc.), maximizing coverage per character.
- Deliberately excludes words already in the name/subtitle (mindset, AI, coach,
  remembers) per Apple's dedup rule.
- Swap candidates once you validate volume: `goals`, `growth`, `focus`,
  `productivity`, `mindfulness`, `gratitude`, `success`, `visualization`.
- Avoid clinical terms (therapy, anxiety, CBT) - they misrepresent the app and
  draw App Review scrutiny.
